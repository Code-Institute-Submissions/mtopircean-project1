Romanian Orthodox Community - Kilkenny, Waterford and surrounding areas


Who we are?



We are a small Romanian community in the Sunny South East of Ireland. We are part of the same Church and united in our love for God and the Orthodox Church. Children and adults united in common prayer, meet every Sunday either in Kilkenny or Waterford for Mass or other Services during the week. Keep an eye on our schedule and planed events in order to participate in the next Service. There are also plenty of events, and people are always open to socialize and welcome one another as part of the large Orthodox family.
